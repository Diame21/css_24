DIFFERENT VARIETIES OF RICE

White Rice: White rice is the most basic form
of rice, typically made from milled,polished
grains. It has a mild flavor and is used as a
base for many dishes or served as a simple side.

Jollof Rice: Jollof rice is a popular West African dish,
particularly in Nigeria and Ghana, known for its vibrant
red color and rich, savory flavor. It's made with rice cooked
in a spiced tomato and pepper-based sauce..

Fried Rice: Fried rice is a dish where cooked
rice is stir-fried in a wok with other ingredients
like vegetables, meat, and seasonings. It's known
for its savory flavor and slightly sticky texture.

Coconut Rice: Coconut rice is made by cooking rice
in coconut milk or coconut water, often with the
addition of other spices. It has a distinct creamy
texture and a subtle coconut flavor.
