DIFFERENT VARIETIES OF RICE

White Rice: White rice is the most basic form
of rice, typically made from milled,polished
grains. It has a mild flavor and is used as a
base for many dishes or served as a simple side.

    Steps on how to prepare

  1. Rinse the rice.
  2. Bring water to a boil, then add the rice. Fill the pot with water (no need to measure), just like you do with pasta water.
  3. Maintain a boil. Boil uncovered without stirring until the rice is softened through but still not mushy.
  4. Drain the rice.

Jollof Rice: Jollof rice is a popular West African dish,
particularly in Nigeria and Ghana, known for its vibrant
red color and rich, savory flavor. It's made with rice cooked
in a spiced tomato and pepper-based sauce..

    Steps on how to prepare

  1. Blend the Base. Using a blender, combine red bell pepper, tomatoes, half of the onion, and scotch bonnet (or habanero). ...
  2. Fry the Tomato Paste + Base.
  3. Season It Up.
  4. Add the Rice + Steam
  5. Fluff + Serve.

Fried Rice: Fried rice is a dish where cooked
rice is stir-fried in a wok with other ingredients
like vegetables, meat, and seasonings. It's known
for its savory flavor and slightly sticky texture.

    Steps on how to prepare

  1. Set your pot on heat and add 200mls of vegetable oil.
  2. Allow the oil to heat, then add sliced onion (1cup), carrot and green beans. Stir all together, add the cabbage and allow to simmer for a minute while you continue to stir.
  3. Add the curry powder and stir evenly (2 tbsp should do), just sprinkle all over and stir. Add the ground pepper, a seasoning cube, chicken stock, and salt to taste.
  4. Taste, if you like the taste then go ahead and add the green peppers, stir and add the pre-cooked white rice. stir all together.
  5. Cover the pot and allow to simmer for 5 minutes,then your food is ready

Coconut Rice: Coconut rice is made by cooking rice
in coconut milk or coconut water, often with the
addition of other spices. It has a distinct creamy
texture and a subtle coconut flavor.

    Steps on how to prepare

  1. Heat up oil and sauté onions and garlic
  2. Pour in the coconut milk and meat stock Allow cook for a minute. Taste for seasoning and adjust to taste
  3. Then pour in the rice and stir.
  4. Cover pot and allow to cook till done. takes about 20 - 25 mins
  5. When the rice is done, turn off heat, stir in the chopped bell peppers, thawed green peas and chopped cooked beef then cover pot to allow steam soften the peas and peppers
  6. Serve warm and Enjoy
